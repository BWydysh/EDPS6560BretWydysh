About me:

I'm a flight respiratory therapist and clinical educator for AirMed at the Univeristy of Utah. My clinical and teaching interests include mechanical ventilation, point of care ultrasound, and any topic my team needs updating on. I moved to Salt Lake City in 2006 from Boston, MA. Along with my job at the University I worked part time with 4Frnt Skis to produce the first 4 generations of the Renegade ski and also the Raven and Wise pro model half-pipe skis. I have been a life long skier and enjoy making turns at Alta and Snowbird.
